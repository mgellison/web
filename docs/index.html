Matt Ellison

Matt Ellison is a research associate at the Hoover Institution. He studied international politics at the School of Foreign Service and is an alumnus of the Roxbury Latin School. He is primarily interested in history and the geopolitical effects of technology, institutions, and ideology.

This website hosts the Gustav Hilger Research Library, which is a collection of documents related to the life of the German diplomat and Soviet expert Gustav Hilger (1886-1965).
