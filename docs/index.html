Matt Ellison

Matt Ellison is Executive Editor of Palladium Magazine, where he oversees the publication's strategy, development, and operations. He is also a researcher, writer, and strategic advisor, with a focus on grand strategy, diplomacy, and the geopolitical effects of technology, ideology, and institutions.

Previously, Matt was a research associate at the Hoover Institution and the Center for International Security and Cooperation (CISAC) at Stanford University. He has completed fellowships at Carnegie Mellon and Georgetown's Institute for the Study of Diplomacy, where he was a Bunker Fellow. Matt has a degree in Foreign Service from the Edmund A. Walsh School of Foreign Service and is an alumnus of the Roxbury Latin School.
