Matt Ellison

Matt Ellison is a research associate at the Hoover Institution and the Center for International Security and Cooperation (CISAC) at Stanford University. He is primarily interested in history and the geopolitical effects of technology, institutions, and ideology. His writing has been published in The National Interest and Palladium Magazine. He studied international politics at the Edmund A. Walsh School of Foreign Service and is an alumnus of the Roxbury Latin School.
