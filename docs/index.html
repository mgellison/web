Matt Ellison

Matt Ellison is Secretary of the American Governance Foundation, a non-profit. From 2021-2023 he was executive editor of Palladium Magazine, where he oversaw the publication's strategy, development, and operations. He researches and writes with a focus on grand strategy, diplomacy, and the geopolitical effects of technology, ideology, and institutions.

Previously, Matt was a research associate at the Hoover Institution and the Center for International Security and Cooperation (CISAC) at Stanford University. He has completed fellowships at Carnegie Mellon and Georgetown. Matt has a degree in Foreign Service from the Edmund A. Walsh School of Foreign Service and is an alumnus of the Roxbury Latin School.
